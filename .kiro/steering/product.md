# Product Overview

## Description

Multi-tenant CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system designed for sales teams and business management. The system enables companies to manage leads, clients, deals, sales pipelines, meetings, tasks, and generate reports.

## Key Features

- **Multi-tenancy**: Supports multiple companies/organizations with data isolation
- **Role-based Access**: Three user roles (Super Admin, Admin/Manager, Agent/Sales)
- **Lead Management**: Track and convert leads to clients
- **Sales Pipeline**: Deal tracking with stages and progress monitoring
- **Client Database**: Comprehensive customer information management
- **Meeting Scheduler**: Calendar integration with reminders
- **Task Management**: To-do lists and task assignments
- **Reports & Analytics**: Performance tracking and sales reports
- **Audit Logging**: Activity tracking for compliance
- **Email Integration**: Automated notifications and email templates
- **Stock/Inventory**: Product and inventory tracking
- **Predictive Analytics**: AI-driven insights and forecasting

## User Roles

- **Super Admin**: System-wide control, tenant management, global oversight
- **Admin/Manager**: Company-level management, user administration, reporting
- **Agent/Sales**: Day-to-day operations, client interactions, deal management

## Business Domain

Sales and customer relationship management with emphasis on multi-tenant SaaS architecture.
